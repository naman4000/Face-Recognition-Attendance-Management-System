Face Recognition Attendance System

Innovative and efficient attendance tracking system using face recognition technology.

Our Face Recognition Attendance System ensures accurate and secure attendance tracking by capturing the student's facial features in real-time. It eliminates the need for manual attendance marking, reducing errors and ensuring reliable records.

Key Features:

  • Secure and accurate identification of students through facial recognition.
  • Automatic attendance logging with time and date stamps.
  • Real-time tracking and reporting of attendance data.
  • User-friendly interface for both students and faculty members.

Implementing this system not only improves the efficiency of attendance management but also reduces the possibility of proxy attendance and ensures data integrity.
